Empowering India's Last Mile with Voice-First AI

AWAAZ ("Haq Mitra") acts as a digital caseworker for citizens who qualify for government help but don't know it exists. By speaking naturally in their native tongue over WhatsApp, rural families are matched against hundreds of welfare programs, instantly generating document lists and pre-filled claims drafts.

How Haq Mitra Works

Bridging the voice-to-paper welfare gap

1
Citizen Speaks naturally on WhatsApp

A rural farmer sends a voice note in Hindi or Tamil describing their situation (e.g. crop status, health issues, pregnancy).

2
AI Parses Demographics & Matches Schemes

The AWAAZ model transcribes the speech, extracts key criteria (age, land, income), and matches them against central/state databases.

3
Caseworker Prints Pre-filled Drafts

Volunteers open the case, verify the checklist, and print the pre-filled official form to finalize submission at local centers.
